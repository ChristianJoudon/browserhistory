Introduction

Web browsers have evolved significantly since the advent of the internet. Learn more about the evolution of web browsers.

A brief history of IE

Internet Explorer was introduced by Microsoft in 1995 and played a significant role in the browser wars.

A brief history of Firefox

Firefox, developed by Mozilla Corporation, was launched in 2002 and became renowned for its speed and efficiency.

A brief history of Chrome

Google Chrome was released by Google in 2008 and quickly became the most popular web browser globally.
